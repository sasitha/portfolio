### Transforming a paper-based trailer rental business into a modern digital operation

This digital transformation project revolutionized how a New Zealand-based trailer rental company operates across 20+ petrol station locations, managing over 1,000 trailers. What started as a manual, paper-based business plagued by lost bookings and revenue leakage became a streamlined digital operation with real-time visibility and automated processes.


## The Challenge

The client faced critical operational challenges with their traditional paper-based system:

- **Lost bookings and revenue** due to manual paperwork errors
- **Poor trailer maintenance scheduling** leading to equipment downtime  
- **Missing trailers** with no system to track overdue returns or overnight holdings
- **Zero visibility** into operations across remote petrol station locations
- **High infrastructure costs** with initial estimates requiring expensive custom kiosks at each location

## The Solution

Leading a team of 8 developers, I architected and delivered a comprehensive digital platform consisting of three integrated components:

### Smart KIOSK Application

Rather than expensive custom hardware, I designed a React Native mobile application that transforms ordinary tablets into powerful self-service kiosks. This decision alone saved the client significant infrastructure costs while providing flexibility to deploy across diverse petrol station environments.

#### Key Features:

- Self-service booking management for customers
- Reduced wait times for both customers and on-site staff
- Cross-platform compatibility running on standard Android tablets
- Offline capability for locations with intermittent connectivity


### Operations Management Portal

A comprehensive web-based dashboard that gives business owners complete visibility and control over their operations.

#### Capabilities:

- Real-time management of 1,000+ trailers across 20+ locations
- Automated maintenance scheduling with proactive service alerts
- Overdue trailer tracking with automated owner notifications
- Comprehensive booking and revenue analytics
- Role-based access control for different user types


### Partner Portal
A dedicated interface allowing petrol station partners to manage trailer information at their locations, ensuring data accuracy and operational efficiency.

## Technical Architecture

- **Frontend:** React (Web Portal), React Native (KIOSK)
- **Backend:** Spring Boot application containerized with Docker
- **Infrastructure:** AWS ECS for scalable container orchestration
- **Database:** PostgreSQL for reliable data persistence
- **Testing:** Comprehensive test coverage using Cypress

The system is designed for high availability and scalability, currently serving live operations across New Zealand with robust monitoring and automated deployment pipelines.

## Business Impact

This digital transformation delivered measurable improvements:

- **Eliminated lost bookings** through digital tracking and confirmation
- **Reduced infrastructure costs** by using tablets instead of custom kiosks
- **Improved maintenance efficiency** with automated scheduling and alerts
- **Enhanced revenue protection** through overdue trailer monitoring
- **Increased operational visibility** with real-time dashboards and reporting
